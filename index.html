About the Event

Run With
Purpose.

Race for First Aid is a one-day track meet built around the iconic 1600m — the mile. Created by Aiden Hayes as his senior project at Columbus North High School, this event brings middle and high school students together to compete, fundraise, and rally around a cause that saves lives.

Whether you're chasing a personal best or running your first mile, this event is for you. Every step, every lap, every second you run puts first aid kits into the hands of local businesses, churches, hospitals, and organizations in our community.

Why We Run

"First aid isn't just a skill — it's the difference between life and loss in the minutes before emergency services arrive."

Every dollar raised goes directly toward purchasing first aid kits to be donated to local businesses, churches, hospitals, and organizations throughout our community. No overhead. No admin costs. Your miles become kits in the places people need them most.
